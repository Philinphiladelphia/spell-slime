// HOW TO USE THIS ASSET PACK //
////////////////////////////////

Here are a few pointers to help you navigate and make sense of this zip file.

- In the root folder, you will find a folder named PNG.

- The PNG folder contains all the props as individual PNGs. Animated props are separated into their own folders.

- The portrait's eyes can follow your player! The direction the portrait's eyes are looking is indicated in the filename.

- Recommended animation FPS: 15 (66.7 ms/frame)
